Jialong Liu (刘家隆) is an incoming M.Phil. student in Artificial Intelligence at HKUST(Guangzhou) with a full scholarship. His research interests span reinforcement learning, embodied AI, and LLM agents.

He graduated with a B.E. in Robot Engineering from Guangdong University of Technology in 2025. As a Research Assistant at PEAK Lab, HKUST(GZ) under Prof. Changhao Chen, he developed the REAL framework for robust extreme agility control of legged robots, achieving zero-shot transfer on the Unitree Go2. He also built Robot-LLM-Align, an end-to-end LLM fine-tuning and ReAct agent system for robot control. Going forward, he is focused on the intersection of LLMs, agents, and embodied intelligence.
