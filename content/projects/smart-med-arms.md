Lab project at GDUT using a JAKA robotic arm with a 6-axis force sensor to detect tissue mass properties through controlled contact.

## Highlights

- Solved forward kinematics (DH method) and inverse kinematics (analytical) in MATLAB.
- Customized ROS control of JAKA arm for precise multi-speed, multi-angle, multi-depth contact experiments.
- Applied LSTM for long-sequence analysis of force sensor data to classify tissue properties.
- Implemented force-feedback closed-loop control based on sensor readings.
