A hybrid robot combining a spherical ground robot with a ducted UAV for emergency response in complex terrain. Developed as part of the Guangdong-Hong Kong Robotics Academy Project Design IV.

## Highlights

- Designed and implemented the full control system framework (communication, sensor, sphere execution, flight control).
- Implemented MPC (Model Predictive Control) rolling control algorithm based on simplified sphere dynamics.
- Integrated Pixhawk 6C Mini for flight control of four ducted motors.
- System enables terrain traversal by rolling and obstacle avoidance by flying.
